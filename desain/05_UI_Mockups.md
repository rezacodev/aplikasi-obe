### REQUIRED TABLES / MODELS (SUMMARY)

- `users`: id, name, email, roles [admin,prodi,dosen], prodi_id, is_active, timestamps
- `prodi`: id, kode_prodi, nama_prodi, ketua_prodi, contact_email, timestamps
- `cpl`: id, kode_cpl, deskripsi, kategori, timestamps
- `cpkm` & `sub_cpkm`: templates for course learning outcomes; mapping to CPL
- `mapping_cpkm_cpl`: sub_cpkm_id, cpl_id, weight, prodi_id, status (draft/prodi_approved/admin_locked)
- `courses`: kode_mk, nama_mk, prodi_id, sks, semester, dosen_id, rps_link
- `instruments`: course_id, title, type, weight, date, mapping (sub_cpkm ids), status
- `scores`/`grade_entries`: instrument_id, student_id, score, grade
- `audit_logs`: action, entity, entity_id, user_id, meta, created_at

Each table must have a management UI: list view, create/edit modal, details, and role-based permissions.

### TABLE VIEWS (UX GUIDELINES)

Common features for management tables:
- Top-left search
- Top-right actions (create, export)
- Column sorting, column visibility menu
- Pagination and page-size selector
- Row actions: Edit, Delete, View
- Bulk actions: Assign Role, Activate/Deactivate, Export

Example columns:
- Users: Name | Email | Roles | Prodi | Status | Last Active | Actions
- Prodi: Kode Prodi | Nama Prodi | Ketua Prodi | Contact Email | Actions
- CPL: Kode CPL | Deskripsi | Kategori | Actions
- CPMK: Kode CPMK | Deskripsi | Bobot | #Sub-CPMK | Actions
- Mapping: Sub-CPMK | CPL | Weight | Status | Created By | Actions
- Courses: Kode MK | Nama MK | SKS | Semester | Dosen | RPS | Actions
- Instruments: Title | Type | Weight | Date | Mapping | Status | Actions
- Scores (per instrument): NIM | Nama | Score | Grade | Î” CPL (inline editable)

---

### WORKFLOWS (HIGH LEVEL)

1. Admin creates users and assigns roles (`admin`, `prodi`, `dosen`), and creates `prodi` entries.
2. Prodi admin configures CPL and CPMK templates; sets program-specific parameters.
3. Dosen adds instruments for their courses and inputs scores; can propose mapping changes.
4. Prodi reviews mapping proposals and approves; approved mappings become active and affect CPL calculations.
5. Admin reviews exports and audit logs; can lock system-wide settings.

---

### DESIGN NOTES

- Mapping lifecycle: draft â†’ prodi_approved â†’ admin_locked. Only `prodi` and `admin` can finalize mappings.
- Role scoping: UI must hide non-permitted actions; show program context (Prodi) when relevant.
- Provide bulk import templates for `users`, `scores`, and `courses`.
- Audit trail required for master data changes and mapping approvals.
- Keep table and form patterns consistent with the component library (shadcn style tokens).

### Tablet View (768px - 1024px)
- Sidebar stays visible
- 2-column grid for statistics cards
- Radar chart at reduced size

### Desktop View (> 1024px)
- Full sidebar + content layout
- 3-column grid for stats
- Full-size visualizations
- Multi-column tables
